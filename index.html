About

A designer for fintech infrastructure.

I focus on the unglamorous core of payments — turning manual, spreadsheet-driven bank operations into auditable, automated consoles, and packaging complex flows into SDKs other teams can drop in and re-skin.

My work spans BillDesk's payments stack — netbanking rails, stored-value wallets, bill-pay, ONDC commerce, and recurring mandates — shipped to production and whitelabelled across banks and partners. I care about operational clarity, design systems that scale by tokens, and interfaces that hold up under real back-office load.
