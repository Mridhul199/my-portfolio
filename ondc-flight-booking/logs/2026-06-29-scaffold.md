# 2026-06-29 — ONDC Flight Booking overview, scaffolded

## What this is
New single-file case study (`index.html`) for an **ONDC flight-booking buyer app**
designed for a bank, with **BillDesk as the bank's TSP**. project-overview skill,
reusing the BillDesk design system `<style>` block (Netbanking/Wallet family).

## Research (web)
- ONDC = Govt-of-India/DPIIT open protocol (not a platform). Roles: Buyer NP,
  Seller NP, Gateway, TSP. TSP = outsourced software provider that lets a buyer
  with no commerce tech join the network. Buyer-App TSP model explicitly targets
  banks/fintechs/telcos. Travel is a newer ONDC category (FICCI MoU).
- Mapped onto our case: bank = Buyer NP, airlines = Seller NPs, BillDesk = TSP
  building the buyer-app booking experience + payment rail. "Built with ONDC."
- Sources: ondc.org, ondc.org/roles-you-can-play, en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Open_Network_for_Digital_Commerce, ibm.com/think/topics/ondc.

## Booking flow (the spine)
Search (oneway/dest/date/travellers) → Results (fare-aware date strip, filters,
sticky total) → Review & traveller (refund-on-cancellation gradient timeline,
form, fare summary) → Add-ons (seat map by price band, meals, per-traveller, live
fare; SpiceMax upsell in wf) → Payment (BillDesk PG: saved/UPI/card/NB/wallet/EMI,
instant discount, coupons). Stepper grew 3→4 steps between wf and VD.

## Asset organisation
By booking step (honours Mridhul's "folders by feature, not before/after"):
assets/search · results · review · addons · payment. Fidelity encoded in
filename: `-wf-` / `-vd-`. So before/after pairs live inside each step folder.
